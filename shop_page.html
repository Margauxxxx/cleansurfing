Let's ride green

Our different products are made from ocean recycled plastic. We recycle net plastic in collaboration with fishermen on the coast and also, all the plastic waste we collect during our beach cleanup. This plastic from the ocean become your surfboard, wetsuit, skateboard or even your sunglasses. Our mission is to provide you performance and great experience for riding and at the same time guarantee you an ecofriendly product. Clean up is the guarantee of a free and green ride.
