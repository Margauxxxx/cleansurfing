Our Ocean Mission

Recycled plastic: from waste to wave

Our mission is based on a sad observation: the plastic ocean. As surfer, the ocean is our playground, as citizen, it is our duty. So, we collect the plastic from the ocean, as well garbages as fishing nets, in order to transform it into surfboard, wetsuit and sunglasses. We organize beach clean up and make partnerships with fishermen. Clean Surfing is a collective project: whether you are a surfer or just an involved citizen, join the movement!
